Design and Illustration

Jyve is a new location-based app for finding community in your city.

I did branding and design for the new location-based app Jyve. I iterated over icon designs and developed one that reflected the spirit of the app with playful illustrations which were simple and provided context for the four main communities in the app Philly, San Francisco, New York, and Chicago. I created iconography, illustrations, and an illustrated splash page to welcome new users and encourage sign-ups.
