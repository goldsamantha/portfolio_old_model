Web Experience

Vuid is a product that allows users to quickly record and share records of the things and people they love.

Vuid wanted a sophisticated web presence that allowed for quickly recording important people and objects, and the ability to connect with other users in the app through messages.

I developed a scheme that allows a user to quickly toggle between consumption and creation. The user can easily message the owner of a page he or she is viewing, while simultaneously viewing the content of the record in question. This allows for users to simultaneously scan a record for talking points and send messages without having to toggle multiple tabs in the browser. If the user wants to go back to observing the full content of the page she can simply close the message panel.

The product needed accessibility for one-click content-creation. I added a new post button in the options panel that allows users to create a new record from any place in the site and easily view collections of records, shown below.

The makers of Vuid wanted records to be easy to create and allow for communities to build up around people who shared similar interests and objects. For instance the way a meet up can be centered around sneakers or brands or artists, Vuid wanted a place for communities to grow around their favorite objects. This means that when someone is writing a post she may wish to reference another similar post and we wanted to make that easier.

Instead of asking a user to open multiple tabs if she wants to observe a post and respond to or reference it, she can do that in one view and three steps, through the use of panels. In just three steps users can make their own record, view, and share it from anywhere in the site.
